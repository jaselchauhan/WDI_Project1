the aim of the game is to turn the board from red to green by pressing on the lites. There's a twist though - each click will toggle the clicked lite and also its adjacent lites green/red. The board needs to be all green to win before you run out of clicks. You have 3 lives and can reset the board if you wish but it'll cost you a life. Good luck!
